Restrictions by a query#

In SQL, this is a query where the WHERE clause includes another `SELECT`` clause.

%%sql

FROM account

LIMIT 5

DeMorgan’s Laws#

NOT (A OR B) == NOT A AND NOT B NOT (A AND B) == NOT A OR NOT B

NOT (A AND NOT B) == NOT A OR B
